⚠ BOL Webmail - PostMessage Command Injection

Cross-Origin Email Exfiltration & Account Manipulation PoC

⚠ Vulnerability Summary

BOL Webmail (bmail.uol.com.br) has a postMessage listener with NO origin validation. Any website can send commands to the webmail and receive sensitive data back, including session tokens, email contents, and account configuration. All responses use postMessage(data, "*").
